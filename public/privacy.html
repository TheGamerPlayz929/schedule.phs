Privacy & Safety

Updated May 25, 2026

Analytics

We use basic, aggregate analytics to see whether the site is working well and which pages are used most. This includes page views and approximate time on page.

We do not collect names, StudentVUE passwords, grades, emails, or personal school records through analytics.

Google Analytics

This site may use Google Analytics. Google Analytics can use cookies or similar identifiers to measure site usage. We have disabled Google signals and ad personalization in our Google Analytics setup.

GradeViewer

GradeViewer is separate from analytics. If you sign in to GradeViewer, your login is used only to request your own StudentVUE data so the dashboard can display it.
